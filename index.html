Hello, I'm Devin Taylor. I'm from Fenton Michigan and love anything tech related. I've done a lot of tech related things from 3D printers to coding robots. Fixing and refurbishing broken electronics is also a hobby of mine. It's not broken until I say so. I used to work at the library Makerspace where I 3D printed, laser cut, cut vinyl, and made clothing for any patron who needed it. Now I work IT at a company called KOSTAL. We manufacture anything you touch in your car, from the steering wheel to the pause button on your radio. Working both these jobs have made me very flexible to whatever gets assigned to me, whether it be making mini 3D printed furniture to helping fix broken software.
