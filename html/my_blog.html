World's Best Video

The video above is a really powerful one. It goes to show how long people will try to accomplish something even with no clear goal in sight. The game they are playing was created by someone with one goal in mind; to waste other peoples time. They log into the custom map and are supposedly trying to solve a riddle, and when the riddle is solved they are allowed access to the theme park that is visible from inside. However there is nothing out there other than a JPEG of a ferris wheel, and the riddle is nonsense. There is no escaping from Mr. Bone's chamber. Some thought can really be put behind why these strangers on the internet are so invested into a farse, what are yours?
